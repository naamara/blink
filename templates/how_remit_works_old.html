How it works

  • 1
    Register.
  • 2
    Enter transaction information.
  • 3
    Delivery.

1. Login or Register

Login to your remit account. If you are new, it only takes a few minutes to verify your email and cell phone number. As soon as that is done, you are ready to send your first transaction.

2. Enter transaction information

Take a moment to create your transaction. Enter the recipient's registered mobile money phone number. Then, enter the amount you want to send and provide your payment information. Remit accepts most major cards including MasterCard and Visa

3. Delivery

After your credit card is successfully charged, you will receive a delivery confirmation email. Your recipient will receive the money directly on their registered mobile money wallet within minutes. Congrats.
